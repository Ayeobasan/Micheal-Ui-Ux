QUID MOBILE APP

Virtual Marketplace for buyers and sellers

My Role

Lead Product Designer

Industry

E-Commerce

Introduction

Quid is an e-commerce platform that offers a seamless shopping experience for both buyers and sellers. With easy-to-use features and a user-friendly interface, Quid makes it simple for buyers to find and purchase the products they need while providing sellers with tools to manage and promote their products. Quid is committed to providing a secure environment for all transactions, so you can shop with peace of mind.

My Role

As the Lead Product Designer on the team, I was responsible for overseeing the entire process from conceptualization and crafting effective design strategies to defining the visual direction of the products for both app and web experiences.

Design Process

Product Research & Discovery

To ensure I design with users in mind, it’s of an important part to understand how shopping experiences have evolved, and how we can make that an advantage to QUID without having to repeat processes over time.  For QUID, we are committed to building for two different users (Buyers and Sellers), connected to just one single account, and this is why the product research has to come in place.

Working alongside the PM assigned to my team, The first approach was to highlight the most important core features that users are most likely to engage with on an e-commerce platform, that provides a smooth shopping experience without glitches and tools every seller needs to list and manage their products.

Buyers

These are users who are more concerned with purchasing items for personal or business purposes

Sellers

These are users who are more concerned with offering goods and services in exchange for money

DISCOVERY INSIGHTS

Every user (buyer) has in mind to shop and buy items online, with the same feel or experiences compared to physically shopping from a supermarket or mart, while every seller also needs the right tool to list and “manage” their products and orders with the same experiences compared to physically selling their products in an office space, without glitches. Here is my summarized summary;

Category pages are very important and plays a huge and vital role on every e-commerce platform as this helps aids users to easily find or navigate the products they need.

Secure environment and process for payments/transactions, with support enabled incase of disputes and payment errors

PProper description of products and clear images of listed products to provide more clarity for buyers to help aid their impressions and thoughts on a product.

TTracking of orders from both parties (Buyers and Sellers), to streamline fulfillment processes, and identifying issues that occurs during shipping and delivery

Orders for both parties (buyers and Sellers) should easily be Orders for both parties (buyers and Sellers) should easily be curated for easy follow through without mixups, to scale up efficiency and speed.

Seamless cart and checkout experiences to streamline and simplifies products purchasing for users, which in turn increases sales and smoothen customer experiences, reducing the chances of cart abandonment

Onboardings

The onboarding helps users understand what can be done on the app and helps inform the users about the app's functions, benefits, and features expectations. For QUID, images were added to aid the content navigation for users while onboarding them.

Registration & Sign In

At these points, users are prompted to enter their personal information and set up a business profile, and security password to safeguard their accounts. Email Verification is done by sending a verification link to the user's email address

In App Navigations

Navigations help to direct the steps of users and help them make choices easily without taking much time. For QIID, personalizing user experiences to tailor their expectations was the top focus, for ease and better experiences.

Product Purchasing for buyers

Product details page is a critical part of an E-commerce experience. To help with users' decisions, key information and quality options for products were added to fast-track user's shopping experiences.

Also, Key information that speaks distinctly to the product was added to give users more clarity and help in their decision-making when wanting to purchase an item. These include Product Features, Delivery and returns Info, Feedback and Ratings, and Seller Information.

Curated Orders

To help users keep track of their orders as an inventory, categorizing the listings was the approach. This way, users will conveniently find their orders putting the different statuses in mind as well, which in turn helps scale up efficiency and speed and reduces confusion.

CArt, Checkout and Payments

For the cart and checkout, we aimed to make it faster and more seamless, so we made every necessary information such as Delivery Details more distinct. The current checkout experience allows users to create more than one shipping address, and make selections on which serves as default shipping address.

Tracking of orders

Users are always curious to know how far or how soon their orders can get to them. To help give users more clarity and also aid the UX of this product, it is important that a status update was made available, and users can get to see the status/progress (Real-time update) of the orders.

Withdrawals and transactions

For every seller who comes to list on QUID, withdrawing their money off should be done conveniently. Every new seller/user, they are first prompted to enter the withdrawing bank info and security key, as a security feature to secure their money from scammers gaining access to their account.

Promotions

Designing for business is an essential factor to be considered. Promotions are an important tool for sellers (Designing for users) to help market their products to a much more volume of audiences so their products can get more visibility. The promotions package on QUID has different plans which in turn, suits different users according to their preferences.

Feedbacks

It is essential to learn more about the experiences of users who use QUID for further improvements on the products. So I designed the feedback channel, as an overlay that pops up automatically on the screen for users to easily share their experiences without much clicks.
