My Role

Industry

Introduction

QUIDPAY is an integrated payment platform/solutions, that offers individual payment solutions that enable users (both existing and new) to pay, receive and also pay for products while offering a safe and secured transaction environments.

My Role

As the Lead Product Designer on the team, I was responsible to oversee the entire process from conceptualisation, crafting effective design strategies to defining the visual direction of the products for both app and web experiences.

Design Process

Product Research & Discovery

Payments generally has been a controversial topic. For QUIDPAY, the goal and focus was to establish an easy-to-pay medium between two different parties, which also includes both buyers and sellers on QUID. Extensively, more options and features like product payments, unique wallet accounts for sellers, and even sending and receiving money. This way, there is more established transparency in payments, and ways to which money is moved between two parties.

DISCOVERY INSIGHTS

Every user (buyer) has on mind to shop and buy items online, with the same feel or experiences compared to physically shop from a supermarket or mart, while every seller also needs the right tool to list and “manage” their products and orders with the same experiences compared to physically selling their products in an office space, without glitches. Here is my summarised summary;

Unique wallet and account number for users to safe keep their funds, and also to receive payments individually.

Secure environment and process for payments/transactions, with support enabled incase of disputes and payment errors

Transaction analytics and real time updates that enables user to keep track of their payment and transaction activities.

High end security measures that protects /safeguards individual account from external access, frauds, scams and threats

In app transaction history for users data keeping to scale up efficiency and speed.

In App Navigations

Navigations helps to direct the steps of users and help them make choices easily without taking much time. For QUIDPAY, faster and quicker options ( Send money, All Request e.t.c) was added for prompt and faster navigations as a part of the home screen features. For transparency and clarity, we wanted to keep users aware about the total amount held in different states (Pending and Disputes). Moving down, transaction history that gives record or history of all transaction activities on the app.

Send Money

Sending money should be one way faster experiences, much more of lesser clicks for the users. For QUIDPAY, we are not limited to intra transfers (QUIDPAY to QUIDPAY), but users can also make transfers or send money to other platforms, hence both email address or account number is an option on QUIDPAY. Email Address was introduced as a payment details, because of users who might want to pay for products, which is another safer medium to secure seller's income.

PRODUCT PAY

Product details page is a critical part of an E-commerce experience. To help with users decision, key informations and quality options for products were added to fast track users shopping experiences.
Also, Key informations that speaks distinctly to the product were added to give users more clarity and helps in their decision making when wanting to purchase an item. These includes, Product Features, Delivery and returns Info, Feedbacks and Ratings, and Seller Information.

Withdraw Money

For users to be able to withdraw, a more secure and safer way of doing that has to be brought in, should incase of third party hack or scammers. To solve this, users have to enter the account information they would always withdraw their funds to, this way, the account information is saved to the system which will be used for subsequent withdrawals automatically.

Online Payment (QR Code)

Online payment is the future of the financial technology, and a more easier way to move money (pay, send or withdraw). For QUIDPAY, the goal is to make the flow and experience more seamless and faster. For each user on QUIDPAy profile, the unique account number is attached to the bar code for the user to share, or copy.
