My Role

Industry

Introduction

QUIDPAY is an integrated payment platform/solution, that offers individual payment solutions that enable users (both existing and new) to pay, receive, and also pay for products while offering a safe and secure transaction environment.

My Role

As the Lead Product Designer on the team, I was responsible to oversee the entire process from conceptualisation, crafting effective design strategies to defining the visual direction of the products for both app and web experiences.

Design Process

Product Research & Discovery

Payments generally have been a controversial topic. For QUIDPAY, the goal and focus were to establish an easy-to-pay medium between two different parties, which also includes both buyers and sellers on QUID. Extensively, more options and features like product payments, unique wallet accounts for sellers, and even sending and receiving money. This way, there is more established transparency in payments and ways to which money is moved between two parties.

DISCOVERY INSIGHTS

Every user (buyer) has in mind to shop and buy items online, with the same feel or experiences compared to physically shopping from a supermarket or mart, while every seller also needs the right tool to list and “manage” their products and orders with the same experiences compared to physically selling their products in an office space, without glitches. Here is my summarised summary;

Unique wallet and account number for users to safe keep their funds, and also to receive payments individually.

SSecure environment and process for payments/transactions, with support enabled incase of disputes and payment errors

Transaction analytics and real time updates that enables user to keep track of their payment and transaction activities.

Transaction analytics and real time updates that enables user to keep track of their payment and transaction activities. threats

In app transaction history for users data keeping to scale up efficiency and speed.

In App Navigations

Navigations help to direct the steps of users and help them make choices easily without taking much time. For QUIDPAY, faster and quicker options ( Send money, All Request, etc.) were added for prompt and faster navigation as a part of the home screen features. For transparency and clarity, we wanted to keep users aware of the total amount held in different states (Pending and Disputes). Moving down, transaction history gives a record or history of all transaction activities on the app

Send Money

SSending money should be a way faster experience, with much more of lesser clicks for the users. For QUIDPAY, we are not limited to intra-transfers (QUIDPAY to QUIDPAY), but users can also make transfers or send money to other platforms, hence both email addresses or account numbers are an option on QUIDPAY. Email Address was introduced as a payment detail, because of users who might want to pay for products, which is another safer medium to secure a seller’s income.

PRODUCT PAY

Product details page is a critical part of an E-commerce experience. To help with users' decisions, key information and quality options for products were added to fast-track users' shopping experiences.
Also, Key information that speaks distinctly to the product was added to give users more clarity and help in their decision-making when wanting to purchase an item. These include Product Features, Delivery and returns Info, Feedback and Ratings, and Seller Information. Product Features, Delivery and returns Info, Feedbacks and Ratings, and Seller Information.

Withdraw Money

For users to be able to withdraw, a more secure and safer way of doing that has to be brought in in case of third-party hacks or scammers. To solve this, users have to enter the account information they would always withdraw their funds to, this way, the account information is saved to the system which will be used for subsequent withdrawals automatically.

Online Payment (QR Code)

Online payment is the future of financial technology and a more easier way to move money (pay, send, or withdraw). For QUIDPAY, the goal is to make the flow and experience more seamless and faster. For each user on QUIDPAy profile, the unique account number is attached to the bar code for the user to share, or copy.
