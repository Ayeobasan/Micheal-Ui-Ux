I'm Michael Olamide, a Product Designer based in Lagos, Nigeria

I am passionate about building sustainable products that yields incredible user experiences, and using these experiences to affect the lives of the users positively.

Having the right mix of creativity, love and passion has led me to finding the right path that greatly defines me, my future and also aligns with it. That chosen path is what i am today, and i have taken as a career - Product Design.

In the last few years, I have worked on various projects across different industry, taking different roles and positions, collaborating and impacting teams positively, through communication, collaboration and leadership.

Outside of my design life, and beyond working as a designer, listening to music is one thing i enjoyed doing most. Also, i enjoy reading and travelling, learning and understand different culture, lifestyles, food, and more importantly, people

One of my goals as a person and a designer, is to impact the world positively years from now, building safe places and environment for everyone, through design and leadership.
